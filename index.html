Angelina Krinos - Engineering Portfolio

Welcome to a collection of my engineering experiments, discoveries, and occasional explosions (controlled ones, of course!). Each project reflects my drive to understand, build, and create solutions for complex systems across energy, aerospace, and beyond. This portfolio is a work in progress and offers a glimpse into my technical journey so far, where I’m blending curiosity, hands-on problem-solving, and a love for discovery into real-world applications.

Selected Projects

  • Stelaris Solar-Powered PCB: Renewable Energy, Simplified - Designed a PCB that harnesses solar power, aiming for clean energy and efficiency in a streamlined, compact design.
  • Robosub Power Systems & Manipulator Arm - Brought together power systems, electronics, and controls for an underwater robot, including designing a custom manipulator arm to interact with its environment.
  • Autonomous Targeting Robot with Firing System - Built an autonomous robot with targeting and projectile-firing capabilities from the ground up in Mechatronics.
  • Precision Audio Amplifier: Boosting Signal Clarity - Created an audio amplifier focused on pure, high-fidelity sound as part of Circuits II, honing my skills in signal processing.
  • Optical Cloaking Device: Making Objects Disappear - Combined theory and practical optics to design a small-scale cloaking device using refraction. Proof that physics can be magical.
  • Rocket Propellant Dynamics & Test Stand - Modeled and tested propellant flow dynamics with MATLAB, then built a test stand to bring the simulation to life.
  • Sapling Satellite Power System - Engineered energy distribution and managed sensor integration for SSI's student-built satellite, bringing power to the skies.
  • 4F Optical System: Spectroscopy for Quantum Materials - Set up a 4F optical system for spectroscopy, helping to advance research on quantum materials and improve experimental results.
  • ISS Zeolite Air Purification: Fresh Air for Space - Developed a 3D-printed filtration material to enhance air quality on the ISS, tackling air purification in extreme environments.
  • Recycled Art Sculpture: Turning Waste into Art - Crafted a sculpture from recycled materials, blending sustainability with art to create a piece that’s both functional and inspiring.
  • BPA Removal in Wastewater: First Steps in Environmental Engineering - Early project focused on reducing BPA in wastewater using adsorption techniques to make a difference in water quality.
