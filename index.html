Angelina Krinos - Engineering Portfolio

Welcome to a collection of my engineering experiments, discoveries, and occasional explosions (controlled ones, of course!). Each project reflects my drive to understand, build, and create solutions for complex systems across energy, aerospace, and beyond. This portfolio is a work in progress and offers a glimpse into my technical journey so far, where I’m blending curiosity, hands-on problem-solving, and a love for discovery into real-world applications.

Selected Projects

  • Zeolite Air Purification for ISS - Created a 3D-printed zeolite material for the ISS to enhance air quality and improve operational efficiency.
  • Projectile-Firing Robot - An autonomous, targeting-capable robot, designed and built in Mechatronics.
  • Audio Amplifier - Built a high-fidelity amplifier focused on signal clarity as part of my Circuits II course.
  • Stelaris Solar Charging PCB - Solar-powered PCB designed for efficient energy management, optimized for renewable applications.
  • Rocket Propellant Dynamics & Test Stand - Designed and modeled MATLAB propellant dynamics and created a test stand for rocketry applications.
  • Sapling Satellite Power Distribution - Engineered power distribution and sensor integration for Stanford’s Sapling satellite project.
  • 4F Optical System - Set up a 4F optical system to enhance spectroscopy capabilities in quantum materials research.
  • Optical Cloaking Device - Developed a device to “cloak” small objects using refraction principles, blending theory with hands-on experimentation.
