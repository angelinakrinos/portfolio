Angelina Krinos - Engineering Portfolio

Welcome to a collection of my engineering experiments, discoveries, and occasional explosions (controlled ones, of course!). Each project reflects my drive to understand, build, and create solutions for complex systems across energy, aerospace, and beyond. This portfolio is a work in progress and offers a glimpse into my technical journey so far, where I’m blending curiosity, hands-on problem-solving, and a love for discovery into real-world applications.

Selected Projects

  • Stelaris Solar-Powered PCB: Efficient Renewable Energy Design - Designed a solar-charging PCB optimized for renewable energy applications and efficient power management.
  • Robosub Power Systems Integration & Manipulator Arm Design - Led the integration of power systems and electronics for an autonomous underwater vehicle, including the design of a robust underwater manipulator arm for object interaction.
  • Autonomous Targeting Robot with Projectile System - Designed and built a projectile-firing robot with autonomous targeting capabilities as part of my Mechatronics course.
  • Precision Audio Amplifier: Signal Clarity in Circuits II - Created a high-fidelity audio amplifier with a focus on enhancing signal clarity and fidelity.
  • Optical Cloaking Device: Experimental Refraction & Concealment - Designed and tested an optical device to “cloak” small objects using refraction, merging theoretical and practical optics.
  • Propellant Dynamics & Rocket Test Stand: MATLAB Modeling - Simulated and tested propellant dynamics using MATLAB and designed a test stand for rocketry applications.
  • Sapling Satellite Power System: Energy Distribution in Space - Engineered power distribution and sensor integration for a satellite project with Stanford Student Space Initiative.
  • 4F Optical System: Spectroscopy for Quantum Materials - Set up and optimized a 4F optical system to enhance spectroscopy for advanced quantum materials research.
  • ISS Zeolite Air Purification: 3D-Printed Filtration Innovation - Developed a 3D-printed zeolite filtration system to improve air quality and efficiency on the International Space Station.
  • Recycled Art Sculpture: Engineering Meets Sustainability - A hands-on project transforming recycled materials into functional art, blending design and sustainability principles.
  • Wastewater Purification: Adsorption of BPA Chemicals - A foundational environmental engineering project focused on removing BPA contaminants from wastewater to enhance environmental health.
